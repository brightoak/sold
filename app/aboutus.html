About Sold.com

Your home, sold your way.

Vision

Empower homeowners with the most comprehensive and trustworthy resources to guide them through their home-selling decisions.

Motivation

No one could have predicted the amount of choices we have today in selling a home. We think the trend of options, choices and variations will continue to expand. Choice abundance is good in principal however, and especially when new, it leads to indecision or worst yet uninformed decision. This observation motivated us to build a selection methodology that is trustworthy, knowledgeable and individualized.

Data Orientation

As you may have experienced, we believe in design and user experience. But we are not just another pretty face; we incorporate analytical models to reconcile biases. Our Soldology© will analyze large number data points to make a scientific recommendation.
