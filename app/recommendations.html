The Art of Negotiations

Agents are objective third parties, who take the emotions out of compromising. They can anticipate the buyer’s demands and are well versed at counter measures.

Legitimacy

A realtor has a legal obligation to act in the seller’s best interest (fiduciary responsibility). They regularly attend seminars to ensure they are compliant with local laws and regulations. A seller doesn’t need to know everything about real estate if they have an agent who does.
